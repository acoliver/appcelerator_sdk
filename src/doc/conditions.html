The Appcelerator Expression Language allows you to unleash the power of Ajax and DHTML without writing any Javascript. As mentioned earlier, Appcelerator expressions take the form of on="[condition] then [action]". The examples below demonstrate our standard conditions.

To prevent a condition from bubbling up to a parent object expecting the same condition append an '!' after the condition name ('click' would become 'click!').
