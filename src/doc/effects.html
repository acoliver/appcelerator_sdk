Scriptaculous Effects

Example | Code

The Appcelerator Platform allows you to leverage Scriptaculous Effects in your application. All Effects are triggered as Actions using our Expression Language - i.e., on="[condition] then [action]". So for effects, you say on="[condition] then effect[effect_name]" and that's it!
